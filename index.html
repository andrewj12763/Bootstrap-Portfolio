About Me

I was born in Lehi and grew up around computers. I worked in several industries ranging from solar to mobile testing. I had many opportunities to grow in the quality control and analysis fields and found QA Engineering to be my career goal.

I love the outdoors, hunting, fishing, and camping. I spend all my free time out in the mountains or exercising for fun. I like to learn foreign languages. I speak Danish the best out of all of them but I understand Swedish, Norwegian, and a little german. I took a few courses on mechanics and love to work on cars especially classic cars.
